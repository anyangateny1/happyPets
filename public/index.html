Happy Pets is a non-profit organisation dedicated to rescuing, fostering, and rehoming abandoned, sick and injured animals around Adelaide.

With your help, we hope to make a difference in the lives of these wonderful animals.

Learn More

How We Help

Animal Rescue

We go on quests to rescue and recover any dog or cat roaming our streets.

Veterinary Services

We provide free veterinary treatment to strays, rescues, and pets.

Fostering and Adoption

We help our rescues find their temporary and forever homes.

How You Can Help

There are countless ways you can help us keep our pets Happy Pets. You can make a donation (however big or small), join our fundraiser events, or volunteer your time and home as a foster parent.
