Why Volunteer?

Volunteering with Happy Pets is a rewarding experience. Not only will you help animals in need, but you'll also meet like-minded individuals and gain valuable skills.

"Volunteering here has been one of the most rewarding experiences of my life." - Jane Doe
"I've learned so much about animal care and met some amazing people." - John Smith

Volunteer Opportunities

Animal Care

Help with feeding, cleaning, and socializing our animals.

Events

Assist with organizing and running fundraising and community events.

Administrative Support

Help with office tasks, data entry, and communications.

How to Get Started

Follow these steps to become a volunteer:

  1. Fill out the application form below.
  2. Attend a volunteer orientation session.
  3. Start making a difference!

Our Volunteers' Impact

In 2023, our volunteers helped us rescue over 500 animals and organize 20 community events.
