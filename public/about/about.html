About Us

Welcome to Happy Pets! We are a passionate team dedicated to providing shelter and care for animals in need. Our mission is to find loving homes for every pet that comes through our doors.

Our Story

Happy Pets was founded by Kemal with the vision of providing a safe haven for animals in need. Since our inception, we have worked tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome countless pets.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide shelter, medical care, and love to every animal that comes through our doors, while our vision is to create a world where every pet has a loving home.

What We Do

At Happy Pets, we rescue animals from various situations, including neglect, abuse, and abandonment. We provide medical care, rehabilitation, and work to find them loving forever homes.
